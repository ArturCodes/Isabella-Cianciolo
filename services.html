CONSULTATIONS

Astrology is meant to be an ongoing conversation. The chart is ever evolving, as are you and I. These dynamics continue to create new potentials that can be seen in the chart. Co-creating with the universe is a beautiful, lifetime commitment. Each consultation will unravel new dynamics that may not have been present, or relevant, during previous conversations. The intention is to bring a sense of grounding and mindfulness to the present moment, in order to meet yourself where you are and empower you to actively participate in the course of your own life, rather than waiting for something to happen.

RELATIONSHIP ASTROLOGY

This session looks at the personality factors of two people in relationship (romantic or platonic, familial or friend, etc) to examine interpersonal relations, as well as an analysis of the potential of the relationship itself, as a separate entity. This is for the purpose of creating more awareness and harmony within the relationship based on individual needs, as well as the combined needs of the partnership. A core understanding of each individual is needed before looking at the relationship patterns with the other.
