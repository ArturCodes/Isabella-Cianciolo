About My Practice

I practice modern, humanistic astrology, focusing on creating a therapeutic healing space that allows for the conversation to emphasize your lived experiences and how they have shaped the expression of your potential as seen through the birthchart. I encourage new clients to tell me about themselves and what brings them to the session, so that we can have a co-creative conversation around where you are in this moment in time, utilizing the birthchart for context.

It is important to note that I am not a licensed therapist, but a student of psychology receiving psychotherapy and mentorship from licensed psychotherapists. Each conversation is transformative— those that I have within my own therapy as well as those that I have with clients through consultations. Astrology is a beautifully reflective tool that opens up a deeper connection to ourselves, as well as others. We'll focus our conversation on which developmental lessons are active at this time in your life based on what you’re experiencing, and we’ll discuss how you might engage with these situations in a matter that reflects and honors your core needs and values.

With astrology, everything is potential— nothing is determined. There are endless variations of the same theme that will emerge, but each moment in time is uniquely separate from the last, offering new opportunities to engage with the cycles of life. The purpose of my sessions are to illuminate the archetypal potentials that exist during this period of transformation, to leave you to further reflect on what resonates with you within the deepest aspects of your soul. The final decision should always be made by yourself, not the practitioner. Listen to what resonates in your heart, for it is your life to live, and you alone uniquely inhabit this moment in time.
