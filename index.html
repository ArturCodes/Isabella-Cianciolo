THE LANGUAGE OF THE COSMOS

Astrology is a language. Each person will interpret it differently, and there are endless nuances to how we can experience the symbolism that lies within the birthchart. Astrology is also a co-creative process, inviting us to work with the symbolism of the cosmos to actualize our own creative path forward. This allows us the opportunity to more consciously engage with our lived experiences through a broadening understanding of our innate nature, our desires, our goals, our conditioning, and the relationships that we experience with others. The natal chart is the template for understanding the soul. Through introspection, we can become more aware of our unconscious tendencies and begin to gently meet ourselves through a more objective lens.

I use astrology as a tool to help facilitate a conversation that, hopefully, leads to enlightenment and empowerment. Through an observation of the energies affecting the natal chart in the present moment, we can discuss the developmental themes that are at play and begin to explore how they are manifesting within your world. Every moment of every day is unique, offering new ways to engage with our lives. Astrology can help to provide guidance through offering a suggestive template of the times.

Free will remains with each of us.

The planets are never doing anything to us, but rather reflecting the current moment through evocative symbolism that embodies the collective experiences shared by all. For example, every human has shared in the experience of having the opportunity to nurture something (another human, an animal, a plant, the Self, etc). These types of shared archetypal human experiences are what the planets represent, and the zodiac delineates the ways in which we can engage with these developmental processes in our own unique ways, creating a guidebook for becoming more conscious of our own, authentic path.
